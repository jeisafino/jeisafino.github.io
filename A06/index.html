About Seventeen

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment. The group consists of 13 members divided into three units: Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance. They are known as a "self-producing" idol group, with the members being actively involved in songwriting, choreography, and other aspects of their music and performances.

Debuting on May 26, 2015, with the EP "17 Carat". Seventeen has since become one of K-pop's most influential groups, known for their synchronized choreography, diverse musical styles, and strong teamwork.
